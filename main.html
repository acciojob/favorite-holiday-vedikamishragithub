Volcanoes for thrill seekers


Well, what’s more thrilling than climbing an active volcano? Mt. Batur and Mt. Agung, the two active volcanoes, are two of the biggest attractions in Bali. Sunrise climb to Mt. Agung’s crater will take approximately five to seven hours, but is worth the pain. Mt. Agung last erupted in 2017!
